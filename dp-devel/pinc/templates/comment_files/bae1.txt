<p>BAE projects have two special rules that always apply, <i>whether or not</i> the general Guidelines currently say the same thing:<br>
* Preserve the page numbers in index, tables of contents, etc.<br>
* Preserve information about all diacritical marks, sub-/superscripts and other special characters. Where possible, use the forms shown in the Proofing or Formatting Guidelines.</p>

<p>For more information about BAE projects, see <a href="http://www.pgdp.net/phpBB2/viewtopic.php?p=50987&start=2">the Uber-Project thread</a>.</p>

<p>For everything else, refer to the <a href="http://www.pgdp.net/c/faq/proofreading_guidelines.php">Proofreading Guidelines</a> and <a href="http://www.pgdp.net/c/faq/document.php">Formatting Guidelines</a>.</p>
